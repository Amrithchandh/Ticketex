Your questions answered

How do I buy tickets?
Browse the events on our platform, select the event you want, and complete the purchase with a few simple steps
Can I resell my ticket?
Yes! You can resell your tickets on our platform, but we have a cap on how much you can increase the price to maintain fairness.
How does the resale cap work?
To prevent ticket scalping, resellers can only sell tickets at a certain percentage above the original price. This ensures fair access to tickets.
Is my payment secure?
Absolutely. We use trusted payment gateways and blockchain technology to protect every transaction.
What is blockchain and how does it help?
Blockchain ensures that every ticket and transaction on our platform is authentic and traceable. It prevents fraud and makes sure that you’re always dealing with legitimate tickets.
